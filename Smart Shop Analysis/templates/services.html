Services

1. Admin

An admin is a user with special privileges who can easily modify the details of all types of users and products. They have full control over the system. As an admin, they can:

  • Manage user accounts and access levels
  • Add, edit, or delete user details
  • Add, edit, or delete product information
  • Modify pricing, discounts, or special offers

2. Vendor

A vendor is a user who can add their products to the system, manage them, and generate bills for customers. Vendors have the following capabilities:

  • Add new products to their catalog
  • Edit or update product information such as description, price, and availability
  • Manage inventory and stock levels
  • Generate bills or invoices for customers

3. Customer

A customer is a user who can view all the products available from different vendors, see special offers, and pre-book items. Customers have the following features:

  • Browse and search for products from various vendors
  • View detailed product information including images, descriptions, and prices
  • See special offers, discounts, or promotions
  • Add products to their shopping cart or wishlist
  • Pre-book items for future purchase
